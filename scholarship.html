The Ultra Black Scholarship

Why We Invest

Supporting the next generation of Black leaders and creators.

The Ultra Black Academic Scholarship & Mentorship Program is our commitment to fostering the growth of Black youth. We aim to support individuals aged 16-25 in their pursuit of higher education and personal development.

We believe that by investing in their potential, we are investing in the future of our entire community.

This program provides more than just financial aid; it's a gateway to mentorship, connection, and opportunity. We award two academic scholarships of $2,000 each, helping to alleviate the financial burdens that can hinder academic success.

Join us in empowering these bright futures.
